🛡️ About CyberSafe

India's Most Advanced AI-Powered Cybercrime Reporting & Detection Platform

Our Mission

CyberSafe is revolutionizing cybercrime reporting in India by combining cutting-edge artificial intelligence with user-friendly design. We empower citizens to report cybercrimes efficiently while providing law enforcement with powerful tools to detect, analyze, and prevent digital fraud. Our platform uses advanced NLP, behavioral analysis, and machine learning to protect millions from online threats.

Platform Features

🤖

AI-Powered Malicious Text Detection

Our advanced Groq-powered AI analyzes emails, messages, and chats to detect phishing, scareware, ransomware, and social engineering attacks. With multi-model fallback and retry logic, we ensure 99.9% uptime and accurate threat classification.

📞

Call Scam Detector

Upload audio recordings or paste transcripts of suspicious calls. Our system uses speech-to-text conversion and NLP analysis to identify scam patterns, urgency tactics, and fraudulent requests in both English and Hindi.

📊

Smart Complaint Management

Automated complaint processing with AI-generated summaries, severity scoring, crime classification, and financial loss estimation. Track your complaint status in real-time with admin notifications and updates.

🔍

NLP-Based Similarity Detection

Using sentence transformers and FAISS indexing, we automatically detect similar complaints to identify crime patterns, serial offenders, and organized fraud networks. Features intelligent caching and batch processing for lightning-fast results.

🎯

Behavioral Analysis & Anomaly Detection

Advanced typing biometrics, IP analysis, VPN detection, and device fingerprinting to identify suspicious login attempts and prevent unauthorized access. Real-time risk scoring with anomaly alerts.

💬

Intelligent Chatbot Assistant

RAG-based chatbot with local PDF knowledge base and web search fallback. Get instant answers about cybercrime laws, filing procedures, and safety tips. Powered by FAISS vector search and Groq LLM for accurate, context-aware responses.

🔐

Multi-Evidence Support

Upload PDFs, images, audio recordings, and videos as evidence. Our platform supports OCR for text extraction from images, audio transcription, and secure file storage with automatic organization.

📈

Advanced Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive admin dashboard with real-time statistics, complaint heatmaps, crime type distribution, severity trends, and user activity monitoring. Export reports and visualize data with interactive charts.

🔔

Real-Time Notifications

Instant notifications for users on complaint status changes and admin notes. Admins receive alerts for new high-severity complaints, suspicious login attempts, and urgent cases requiring immediate attention.
